PROJECTS PAGE

Internet of Things (IoT)

Our cohort made their own devices for the IoT Final Project. They were able to present them at Google, which was very interesting.

TinkerCAD is a website, where people can create their own devices online. When our cohort uses the website, they use tools to create devices online, including lights, wires, and buttons.

An Arduino is a device that is used to facilitate the function of a device. It could used online, such as in TinkerCAD, or as a physical object, in which it could be connected to other components using physical objects, such as a breadboard and wires.
IT Essentials

During the Break and Build event, our trainees broke the PC into parts and then built it back. They were able to see how the parts worked and how their functions combine to have a PC working.

During the Cables and Crimping event, our trainees learn how to create cables. They do this by cutting some parts of the cable and arranging the small lines inside it. Then they crimp the cable and connect it to test if the connection works.

Our trainees learned the ticketing system, in which it uses tickets to organize each customer that needs service. The ticketing system is used when it comes to areas, such as IT Support. It also is an effective way to support customers and their needs.
Programming

The Web App is a final project for the Programming section of the program. Our trainees built their own websites that are easy to use and that they included several interesting features that made the websites extraordinary.

The About Me Page is a project that shows information describing the person. Our trainees built their own pages that show details about who they are, their careers, what projects they made, etc.

The Lyrics Page is a project that shows the lyrics of a song. Our trainees built their own pages that show the lyrics of a song that they like or that they enjoyed listening to.
Critical Career Skills

Our cohort present the business communication project by different groups. Each group presents their own topic, which is related to technology. Then, they present the details that support their topic.

Our cohort visits LinkedIn to learn more soft skills for their careers. They were able to get a professional headshot that they can use for their LinkedIn profiles. They learned about different critical career skills, including networking and endorsing.

Our cohort does mock interviews, in which they practice interviews with someone in order to gain skills when it comes to getting a job. They did this in Wells Fargo at San Leandro.
